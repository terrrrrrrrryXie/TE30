Suggested Materials

  • Tightly woven fabrics (denim, canvas, polyester blends).
  • UPF-rated clothing for extended outdoor time.
  • Darker or bright colours can block more UV than pale colours.

Outfit Recommendations

  • Wide-brimmed hat and UV-blocking sunglasses.
  • Long-sleeved shirt and long pants when UV is high or extreme.
  • Lightweight, breathable fabrics to stay cool while protected.
