Understanding UV Levels

Learn what the numbers mean for your skin. The UV Index runs from 0 to 11+. Higher values mean greater risk of harm from unprotected sun exposure. At 11 (Extreme), protection is essential—seek shade, wear sunscreen SPF 50+, and cover up.
